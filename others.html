Jio Mart

 RIL is accelerating the rollout of its new commerce initiative JioMart to cover other sectors including electronics, fashion, pharmaceutical and healthcare.

 JioMart is available in 200 cities and is clocking over 250,000 orders a day. In the coming years, it will cover many more cities, and expand to many more categories.

 JioMart is a tech-enabled partnership that will link producers, traders, small merchants, consumer brands and consumers.
